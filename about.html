About Me

Allow me to introduce myself...

BIO

Hi! I'm Robby, a web developer with a passion for quality. I enjoy understanding and developing systems with an emphasis on simplicity. In my free time I enjoy sports (GO BLAZERS!), listening to podcasts, and playing all kinds of games. You can usually find me riding around Portland Oregon with a cribbage board riding shotgun.

Customer Focused

My experience includes over seven years of testing experience with a wide range of platforms including Xbox, iOS, Android, Front-End, and Back-End web applications. I have developed a keen sense for customer expectations, priorities, and values. I’ve also cultivated an eye for detail, a devotion to quality, and a practical understanding of risk assessment.

Team Player

I’ve worked with remote teams around the world, enjoyed the startup into acquisition journey, and have contributed to teams in multiple phases of the software development lifecycle. Through all this a few things remain clear to me, communication is key, everyone is on the same team, and don’t underestimate the power of team morale.

Technical Aptitude and Desire to Learn

My curiosity for design and technology has led me on a quest from 3D animator, video game tester, software quality engineer, and most recently full stack web developer. My latest accomplishment is alumni of the amazing Free Code Camp curriculum! It was a blast and I learned a ton of skills which I can’t wait to leverage to bring a ton of value into the world.
